# AURA VILLA UI/UX Design Blueprint

## Product Standard

AURA VILLA is designed as a private digital advisory experience, not a commodity property marketplace. The interface uses cinematic media, restrained typography, lifestyle-led search, and quiet conversion paths to create trust with high-net-worth buyers, renters, owners, and investors.

## Visual Identity

- Base palette: Deep Obsidian `#070707`, Alabaster `#F8F6F1`, Ivory `#EFEAE1`, Slate `#6E7377`
- Accent palette: Royal Champagne Gold `#D4AF37`, Muted Bronze `#9C7A45`
- Headings: Cormorant Garamond editorial serif
- Body: Inter sans-serif
- Layout: generous whitespace, full-bleed imagery, editorial sections, borderless property cards
- Motion: slow reveals and hover states using `cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)`

## Architecture

- Home: cinematic brand introduction, lifestyle search, featured collections
- Residences: advanced catalog, map panel, lifestyle filters, privacy filters, currency conversion
- Property Narrative: media-led detail section, specs, interactive floor-plan preview, POA positioning
- Concierge: viewing, rental, investment, and owner consultation booking
- Owner's Vault: owner dashboard preview with yield, occupancy, maintenance, and document status
- Insights: editorial credibility layer for investors, VIP renters, and owners

## Conversion Features

- One-click Private Advisor CTA
- WhatsApp Business entry point
- Premium video call scheduling placeholder
- Lifestyle-driven filtering
- POA pricing behavior
- Currency conversion for USD, EUR, AED, and GBP
- Comparison drawer for up to three residences
- Owner dashboard surface for luxury asset management

## Accessibility And Performance

- Semantic landmarks and form labels
- High contrast dark and ivory sections
- Keyboard-friendly buttons and links
- Reduced-motion support
- Lazy-loaded catalog images
- Static dependency-light architecture for fast local loading
